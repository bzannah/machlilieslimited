Questions

Frequently asked.

What an AI & technology consultancy actually does — and how working with Mach Lilies works.

What does Mach Lilies do?

Mach Lilies is an independent AI and technology consultancy. We design, engineer and scale software for ambitious teams — spanning applied AI and machine learning, cloud and platform engineering, data and analytics, product engineering, design, and technology strategy.

What is an AI consultancy, and what does an AI consultant do?

An AI consultancy helps organisations identify, build and deploy artificial-intelligence and machine-learning systems that deliver real business value. At Mach Lilies that runs from LLM and generative-AI applications, ML pipelines and intelligent automation through to MLOps — designed, shipped to production, and measured against outcomes rather than slideware.

What AI and machine learning services do you offer?

We build LLM and generative-AI products, retrieval and agent systems, custom machine-learning models, data and ML pipelines, and intelligent automation. Engagements span AI strategy, rapid prototyping, production engineering, and MLOps for reliability at scale.

Which industries do you work with?

We work across FinTech, HealthTech, logistics, retail and commerce, and B2B SaaS — with both venture-backed startups and established enterprises. The practice is global and fully remote, working across time zones.

How do engagements and pricing work?

Most engagements start with a free consultation and a short, fixed-scope discovery to frame the problem, followed by milestone-based delivery. Every project is staffed by senior principals, and we scope pricing to outcomes rather than headcount. Get in touch for a tailored proposal.

What makes Mach Lilies different from other technology consultancies?

We're a small, senior, founder-led studio — the people who pitch are the people who build. We take few clients on purpose, ship production-grade work from day one, and engineer our own hand-off so you own clean, documented systems that outlive the engagement.

Do you work with startups or enterprises, and are you remote?

Both. We partner with early-stage startups needing a fractional CTO or first AI product, and with enterprises modernising platforms or adopting AI. Mach Lilies is a global, remote-first studio.

How do I get started?

Email machlilieslimited@gmail.com with a few lines about what you're building. We reply to every serious enquiry within one business day and offer a free first consultation with no commitment.

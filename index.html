Questions

Frequently asked.

What agentic operations means in practice — and how working with Mach Lilies works.

What does Mach Lilies do?

Mach Lilies is an independent agentic operations and AI engineering consultancy. We design, deploy, govern and operate safe AI agent workflows that perform real business processes — with secure integrations, human oversight, monitoring, evaluation, audit trails and measurable ROI. The same senior team also delivers AI consulting, modernization, cloud, data and strategy.

What is agentic operations?

Agentic operations is the design, deployment and ongoing operation of AI agents that perform real business workflows — bounded systems that read, draft, classify, update and route work across your tools, with human approval for high-risk actions and a full audit trail. It is the difference between an AI demo and an AI workflow that runs in production.

Are the AI agents fully autonomous?

No — and that is deliberate. The strongest production systems are bounded: agents get least-privilege access to only the tools and data a workflow needs, humans approve high-risk actions, and every step is logged. We design for control and human oversight, not uncontrolled autonomy.

What is the Agentic Operations Sprint?

It's our flagship engagement: a focused, fixed-scope sprint that maps your highest-value AI agent workflows, prototypes the strongest one against real inputs, and produces a production rollout plan with governance, integrations, ROI and risk controls. It's the clearest first step from AI experiments to agentic operations. See the Sprint →

How is the ROI of AI agents measured?

Before we build, we estimate the time saved, error reduction and throughput gain. After launch, monitoring and evaluation track real usage and cost, so the operational impact is measured rather than assumed — and reported back to you.

Which systems can the agents work with?

We build secure, least-privilege connectors into the systems a workflow touches — typically CRM, ERP, email, document storage, ticketing, finance and internal tools. Access is scoped to the specific workflow and nothing more, and every action is logged for audit.

What makes Mach Lilies different?

We sell governed AI operations, not billable engineering hours. We're a small, senior, founder-led practice; we build monitoring, evaluation, permissions and human oversight into delivery rather than as an afterthought; we're model-agnostic; and we hand over clean, documented, owned systems with no lock-in.

Email machlilieslimited@gmail.com with the workflow you want to improve, the systems involved, the current manual effort, and any risk or compliance concerns. We reply to every serious enquiry within one business day, and most engagements begin with an Agentic Operations Sprint.
